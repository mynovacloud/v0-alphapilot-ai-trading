How the bot learns
  1. Decide. The technical engine produces a signal; Claude reviews it with the full playbook and recent history before choosing an action.
  2. Act. The trade is opened in paper mode through the existing risk manager. All caps still apply.
  3. Reflect. When the trade closes, Claude writes a verdict (good_call / bad_call / lucky / unlucky / neutral) and a -1..1 process-quality score.
  4. Remember. Up to 3 generalizable lessons per reflection are added to the playbook with weights.
  5. Consolidate. Periodically the playbook is compressed: duplicates merged, contradictions resolved, weights re-ranked.
  6. Graduate. Once the readiness score crosses 75 with a healthy sample size and win rate, you can flip a wallet to live_shadow and finally to live.
